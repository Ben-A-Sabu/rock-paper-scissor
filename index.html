Rock Paper and Scissors

How to play

Rock Paper and Scissors is a game where you have to choose between rock, paper and scissors.

The computer will randomly choose between rock, paper and scissors. You have to choose between rock, paper and scissors.

If you choose rock and the computer chooses scissors, you win. If you choose scissors and the computer chooses rock, you lose. If you choose paper and the computer chooses rock, you win. If you choose rock and the computer chooses paper, you lose.

If you choose the same thing as the computer, it's a draw.
